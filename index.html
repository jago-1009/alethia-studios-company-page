Welcome to Alethia Studios

At Alethia Studios, we believe in the power of great stories—Aletheia (ἀλήθεια), the Greek word for “truth,” guides everything we create. creating imaginative worlds, exciting adventures, and memorable experiences for everyone to enjoy. Our mission is to craft media that offers hope and meaning, From games and animation to immersive experiences, we love building worlds that spark curiosity and bring people together. We’re always open to creative partnerships—reach out and let’s make something impactful together.

About Our founder

Imani Peters is the founder of Alethia Studios, a multimedia company dedicated to creating innovative games and experiences that inspire and connect. She graduated from Indiana University with a Bachelor’s degree in Media Arts, specializing in Game Design and 3D Graphics & Animation. With extensive experience in project management and a passion for collaboration, Imani thrives in leading multidisciplinary teams to bring creative visions to life. Drawing on her passion for storytelling, visual artistry, and interactive experiences, Imani is building Alethia Studios as a space for imaginative worlds and meaningful narratives to thrive.
